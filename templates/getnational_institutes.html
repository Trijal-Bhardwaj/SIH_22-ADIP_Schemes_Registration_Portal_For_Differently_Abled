Disablity Specific Schemes

Quantum of Assistance 

For providing modern assistive devices for all categories of PWD both physical and mental and multiple disability impaired groups, e.g. DAISY Book Players and other Talking Devies, Net Book Laptop and Digital Magnifiers for visual impairment and behind the ear (hearing aid) for hearing impairment, the items will be decied by an Expert Commitee constituted in the Department of Disability Affairs with the approval of Minister for Social Justice and Empowerment..

Category-wise Kits 

Category-wise Kits for Visually Impaired, showing indicative prices and source of procurement as at Annexure-II. These are Kit-1: For Primary School children studying in class-1 to class-5, Kit-2: For Upper Primary School children studying in class-6 to class-8, Kit-3: For Senior Secondary School children studying in class-9 and 10, Kit-4: For Students studying in class-11 and 12 which has 2 sub-parts viz. Kit-4 (A) for Blind Students and Kit-4 (B) for low vision Students, Kit-5 : For College Students which has 2 sub-parts i.e. Kit-5 (A) for Blind Students and Kit-5 (B) for low vision Students and Kit -6 : ADL kit for Adults.

Braille Watch/Quartz Braille watch (ladies and gents) Disabilities 

Quartz watch with two hands for minutes and hour, easy to open lid from 6 o'clock position, Combination of two tactile dots to denote numbers 3,6, and 9; combination of three tactile dots for number 12 and single tactile dot for rest of the numbers, made of high grade stainless steel, leather strap.

Signature guide with rupee checker and wallet & Research 

Set of two non-electronic pieces that would help with blindness identify currency, Piece one: Made of rigid plastic with signature guide slot and cuts for currency measurement that fits easily in pocket, Piece two: Made of flexible material with cuts for currency measurement that fits will in both men and women wallets. Both pieces must identify all denominations from Rs5 to Rs1000 To be supplied with matching wallet for women.

Spectacles Disabilites 

Spectacles Frames with metallic and spring loaded frame fitted with English white lenses/glass(+) different numbers. Spectacles cover and cleaning cloths are free of cost.

Refreshable Braile Display Disabilities 

Refreshable Braile Display System 1) To be able to display at least 40 Braile Cells at a time.
2) Connectable to the Computer via USB port and Bluetooth.
3) Inbuilt rechargeable Battery.
4) Must have at least 8 configurable navigation Keys.
5) Must have one cursor routing key per Braille Cell.
6) Must have brailler type six keys for typing commands or text on computer.
7) Should be compatible with open source screen reading software for windows and Linux.

Talking Thermometer 

1) Will announce the body temperature in clear audible voice.
2) Should have the option of temperature announcement in Degree Celsius or Faranheit.
3) Should have an option of Automatic switch-off.
4) All the funtionality of the device should be accessible through clear audbile voice.

Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India  (ALIMCO)

ALIMCO is a Schedule 'C' Miniratna Category II Central Public Sector Enterprises, registered under Section 8 (Not for Profit motive) of the Companies Act 2013, (corresponding to Section 25 of the Companies Act, 1956) is functioning under the Administrative Control of Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities. It is 100% owned Govt. of India Central Public Sector Enterprises with an objective of benefiting the persons with disability to the maximum extent possible by manufacturing Rehabilitation Aids for persons with disabilities and by promoting, encouraging and developing the availability, use, supply and distribution of Artificial Limbs and other Rehabilitation Aids to the disabled persons of the country.
